ABOUT US

Our mission is to create a fun and valuable environment that is valuable for developers. This generates value for device and OS makers, carriers and consumers.

As developers for more than two decades, the Dev-LABS team understands the pain associated with development processes, and understands how frustrating and time-consuming it can be. Device-based testing is one of the most notorious as it is not only frustrating and time-consuming, but also expensive.

However we also know that consumers prefer different types of devices that best fit their needs and experience. Therefore, this device-related environment will remain fragmented and evolve with new types and models year after year making it even more challenging for developers.

Hence we founded Dev-LABS!
